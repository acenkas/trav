How to win any girl

With every guy, it happens: you liked a girl, you talked to her a couple of times and wanted to start a relationship with her. But suddenly you will find out that there are several men like you who want to win a girl. And here the question arises: what to do in this situation, how to get around everyone and take this prize? Today we will talk about this:

Competition

When a man begins to make every attempt to prove to a woman that he is the best among other men: he gives expensive gifts, takes her to restaurants and tries to devote all his free time only to her. Then it makes a mistake to enter into a contract with the customer. the ranks of the rest of the suitors. Roughly speaking, you begin to measure your dignity with others, which does not play into your hands in any way. A woman in this situation gives only pleasure and satisfies her self-esteem, because it is so popular among men. Therefore, you should not do this.

Their game

When you don't join the if so, they must play their own game. That the girl would understand that you are special and that you do not need to prove something to her to interest her. To do this, do not devote all your time to her, go where you want, not her. Don't give in to her whims. A woman should see and feel in you a worthy male who will protect and help her. And he wouldn't always go along with her.

Act as if it's already yours

When a girl wants to win a lot of men, and she does not start a relationship with anyone. So waiting for the only one who will be cooler and more worthy of them all. @ when Dating, show that it is already yours. Be at ease with her. Talk about your plans where she already appears without her consent. As if that's how it's supposed to be. And soon it will be.

If these methods did not work, then perhaps the girl is not looking for a relationship, but likes to play, and lead men by the nose, emphasizing its importance.
