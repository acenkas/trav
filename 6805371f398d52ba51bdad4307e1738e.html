What every girl secretly dreams of, but won't tell the guy about it

Almost every girl wants a guy to somehow surprise her with unexpected gifts. For example, give her flowers for no reason, watch her favorite movie, or take her to a place she has long wanted to go. Your relationship will become even stronger, and the girl will be on the I'm over the moon.

A relationship without fights is not a relationship. But they can be avoided or the conflict can be resolved quickly. When you are emotional, she can say something without thinking. That's not what I meant at all. It may be stereotypical and hackneyed, but there is no escaping classic romance. The girl will be very surprised, and will be glad to see what you have prepared for her. Girls like it when they are told they love you. But guys mostly do this either at home, or over time completely forget to say nice words. They want it never to end.
