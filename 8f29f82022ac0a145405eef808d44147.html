6 rules for meeting her parents

No matter how much you want, you will still have to meet your girlfriend's parents. The sad thing about this is that they may not like you, and your relationship may end quickly. Therefore, it is important to prepare well for getting to know them.

First impression

Wherever you are, a cafe, a job interview, or a date, a person is always met by their clothes. First impressions are always very important in any meeting. Dress neatly and neutrally, without holes in your shorts, t-shirts, or crumpled clothing, so you will immediately spoil your impression. Also shave before the meeting, moms girls always pay attention to this. But do not dress in a strict suit, so you may simply not be believed.

The first acquaintance

Perhaps this is a frequent question from people: where did you meet? And parents are no exception, and they will also ask it. The answer is like, "we met at a club, started fucking, and then had sex sex in the toilet", they will not be very satisfied. It is better to discuss in advance with the girl that you will answer this question. Or maybe she's already told them something about you. This way you will avoid awkward situations.

Collect information

Meeting a girl's parents is like an interview with a large company. So that it is better to know in advance: who they work for, what they do in their free time, whether they are divorced or not, and so on. With this information, you can easily find a common language and please them. But you can tell them a bad story and they won't be impressed at all.

Please her mother

Mom really it plays an important role in a girl's life, and her opinion is more important than that of her father. And if you do not please her in any way, then expect trouble. To win her trust, pay a compliment about the food and ask for a recipe for some dish. This will give you + 50 to the charm on her part. Then after the meal, help clear the dishes from the table and offer to wash them. Of course, she will tell you that she will wash everything herself. But this gesture she will remember and then tell the girl what a gallant and caring guy you are. A good relationship with your mother will protect you from attacks not only from the girl, but also from the whole family.

Don't forget your father

The idea that you are sleeping with his daughter pleases me it is the least of all. So any father will be initially skeptical about you. In order to turn things around, find out in advance about his work and Hobbies.
And prepare a couple of questions or interesting stories related to them. So you will spend a couple of hours talking to him, and become his ally. Just not lie that you like everything he does.

The present and future plans

Don't forget to buy flowers for her mother, and a nice bottle of wine. So you will immediately present yourself in a good light to them. A girl will not bring just anyone to her parents ' house, she will think 1000 times before doing it. So if you're already in her house, so she has big plans for you. And she doesn't want to fall in the mud with her face, and will wait for you to tell her that you have big plans for a future together and that you plan to get married later. This way, parents will not worry that you are using it, and will only be happy.
